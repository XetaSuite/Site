This privacy policy describes how the **XetaSuite** mobile application collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

## 1. Application Publisher

The XetaSuite mobile application is published by **XetaSuite** (hereinafter "we", "us").

---

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1 Authentication Data

To access the application, you must authenticate with a XetaSuite account. Session data is stored securely on the device using **Expo SecureStore** (AES encryption).

No password is stored in plain text on the device.

### 2.2 Audio Data (Microphone)

The application uses your microphone **only** for the integrated voice assistant.

**What happens during a voice session:**

1. You manually activate the voice assistant by pressing the dedicated button.
2. Audio is captured on the device and sent to the platform's **speech recognition API** (Android Speech Recognition API / Apple Speech Recognition API).
3. The resulting text transcription is sent to the XetaSuite API for processing.
4. Raw audio is **never sent** to XetaSuite servers.
5. Raw audio is **not stored** on the device or in the cloud.

**What we do not do:**
- We do not record your conversations.
- We do not transmit audio to third parties (beyond the platform's native speech recognition API).
- We do not use audio for advertising or behavioral analysis purposes.

### 2.3 Usage Data

Actions performed in the application (viewing materials, incidents, maintenances, etc.) are transmitted to the XetaSuite API as part of normal service operation. This data is linked to your user account.

---

## 3. Required Permissions

### Android

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO` | Audio capture for the voice assistant |
| `android.permission.MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS` | Audio mode management during voice sessions |
| `android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Keep audio session alive in the foreground |
| `android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK` | Foreground voice response playback |

### iOS

| Info.plist Key | Purpose |
|---------------|---------|
| `NSMicrophoneUsageDescription` | Audio capture for the voice assistant |
| `NSSpeechRecognitionUsageDescription` | Speech recognition by the iOS system |

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing is based on your **explicit consent**:
- For the microphone: you grant permission the first time you use the voice assistant. You can revoke it at any time in your device settings.
- For authentication and usage data: it is necessary for the performance of the XetaSuite service contract.

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Session data (token) | Duration of the session, deleted on logout |
| Captured audio | Not retained (real-time processing only) |
| Navigation / action data | According to your organization's policy (site administrator) |

---

## 6. Your Rights

Under the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, you have the following rights:
- **Right of access** to your data
- **Right to rectification**
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to object** to processing
- **Right to data portability**

To exercise these rights, contact your XetaSuite administrator or the application publisher.

---

## 7. Security

Your data is protected by:
- Encryption of authentication tokens (Expo SecureStore / AES)
- Encrypted HTTPS communication between the app and the API
- Data access restricted by your roles and permissions in XetaSuite
